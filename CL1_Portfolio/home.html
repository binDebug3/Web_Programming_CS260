My Story

What is the Applied and Computational Math Emphasis (ACME) at BYU?

ACME is a unique and intensive intersection of advanced mathematics, statistics, and computer science designed to prepare people to solve 21st century problems. As the foundation for modern technology, a deep understanding of mathematics enables successful algorithm design, effective data analysis, and innovative solutions to modern challenges.

Why Did I Choose ACME?

When I first started my college education, I was an electrical engineer. I really enjoyed my classes, but as I learned more about the possibilities that math enables like machine learning and data analysis from friends and information sessions, I considered the idea of switching my major. It was hard to let go of my plans to become an engineer, but I get so excited learning about the power that math has to teach a computer to learn and help us understand the world better, that I knew I needed to change my direction.

What is the Data Science and Machine Learning Concentration?

Every student in ACME chooses a concentration in addition to their regular math coursework. I choose Data Science and Machine Learning because my dream is to be able to use those tools to improve people's quality of life, help people make better decisions, and allow people to work more efficiently. So, in addition, to my normal classes, I am also taking severl classes on statistics, computer science, and machine learning techniques.
